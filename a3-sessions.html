About This Activity

  

  

    

      

        
Presented by Dan Clark, Nitro of American Gladiator fame. Our biggest, baddest, muddiest event ever. You'll battle mud, fire, ice as you forge your way through 25 obstacles over 5 miles of diverse terrain. Gladiators will climb sheer walls, slither across muddy fields crisscrossed with barbed wire and rocket down a 150 foot water slide into a pool of mud. 

You'll bound through windows of a broken down paintball fortress and scamper across a concrete jungle littered with the remnants of countless battles  among other obstacles  before you conquer the Beast then charge through the finish line hands thrust in the air in glorious triumph proving once and for all you are a GLADIATOR BADASS. 

After the event, you'll celebrate like a Gladiator God and rejuvenate your weary, mud-splattered soul with beer, food and rock'n roll while you share your spoils of victory with fellow Gladiator combatants.
 
DISTANCE:
 
Super Gladiator - 5 Miles/ 25 Obstacles
 
 
 
Regular Gladiator-3.5 miles/19 Obstacles

 
Saturday SUPER GLADIATOR Waves 8:30am (Competitive), 9:00am, 9:30am, 10:00am
 
Saturday Gladiator 5K Waves 10:30am, 11:00am, 11:30am,12:00pm, 12:30pm
 


 
EACH SURVIVOR RECEIVES: 
 
 
 
1. One Beer. * (must be 21)
 
2. Live band.
 
3. Rock'n Rumble Pit.
 
4. Gladiator Tough Finishers Medals.
 
5. Cool Gladiator Rock'n Run T-shirt.
 
6. Respect, props, and fist bumps
 
7. The most insane day of your life.
 
8. A memory that will echo into eternity.
 
9. Bad-ass post race party.
 
 
 
* Each participant receives one free beer. Don't be an idiot. Don't drink and drive.
 
 
 
CHECK OUT WWW.GLADIATORROCKNRUN.COM FOR MORE INFO!

                      What is the ACTIVE Advantage trial membership?
                    
                  

                    
                      
                        
                      
                        
                    
                

                

                  

                    

                      The 30-day trial of the ACTIVE Advantage membership allows you to check out the program for yourself before starting a full annual membership. During this trial period you have full access to member benefits, including all ACTIVE registration discounts, access to free event entries, gear discounts and more. If you decide during the trial period that you don't want to continue the membership, just let us know. Otherwise, at the end of your 30 day trial we will extend your member benefits for a full year at the current annual membership fee.

That's less than $6 per month to enjoy the program all year round.

                      How do I redeem ACTIVE Advantage registration discounts?
